Introduction

Go Listen respects the privacy of all visitors to our website. This Cookie Policy outlines our use of cookies on golisten.ucd.ie

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

How We Use Cookies

We use only 1st party cookies which are strictly necessary (essential cookies) for the operation of this website. We only use cookies to make our site faster and easier to use.

As we only use essential / strictly necessary cookies, which do not require user consent, we do not need to make use of a cookie consent management platform here.
